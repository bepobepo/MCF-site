Mooncow Fest is a communal festival (gathering?) of around 80–150 friends, friends-of-friends, and lovely strangers — pitched on a working farm in East Sussex. There are no headliners, no wristbands, no sponsors. Just people, fields, fire, music, food cooked together, and the occasional cow.

The event is co-created by the participants. Bring yourself, bring your ideas, your music, and anything you'd like to share — a workshop, a talk, a DJ set, food, anything!

We've been doing this since 2020. Each year is a little different. The shape comes from the people who show up.

~ the land ~

Rolling hills,
a quiet farm,
big skies.

We pitch up at Great Danegate Farm, near Eridge Green in East Sussex — about an hour by train from London Bridge to Tunbridge Wells, then a short hop. There's space to camp, a kitchen tent, a fire, a dance tent, and a lot of green.

Getting there by car, public transport, or carpool. We'll coordinate lifts in the run-up — let us know on the sign-up form if you'd like a seat, or have one to offer.

~ pack list ~

What to bring.

A rough kit for a comfortable weekend. Once you've signed up, we'll share the full list and the shared kitchen / equipment sign-up.

14 items · < 25kg

  1. Tent + sleeping bag (10°C-rated)
  2. Sleeping mat for under your bag
  3. Layers — it gets cold at night
  4. Waterproof jacket
  5. Hiking boots or wellies
  6. Headtorch (better than a regular torch)
  7. Water bottle + 5L spare
  8. Cup, plate, cutlery
  9. Towel + biodegradable shower gel
  10. Swimwear
  11. Toilet paper
  12. Mosquito repellent
  13. Charged power banks
  14. Snacks to share
